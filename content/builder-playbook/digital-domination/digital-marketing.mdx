# Digital Domination: Building Your Online Presence

<Lead>Door-to-door will always be your primary lead source. But digital presence matters for social proof, lead generation, team credibility, and referral amplification. You don't need to be an Instagram influencer — just be easy to find online and look professional.</Lead>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Digital Reality Check">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "60-70%", label: "Leads from Door Knocking" },

  { value: "5-10%", label: "Leads from Digital" },
  { value: "20-30", label: "Extra Appointments/Month from Digital Done Right" }
]} />

<KeyTerm term="The Real Value">Digital is supporting, not primary. But that 5-10% can mean 20-30 extra appointments per month. Plus, when prospects Google you and see great reviews and professional presence, they're more likely to say yes at the door.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Digital Presence Checklist">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Google Reviews (Most Important)">

First place people look. Affects search rankings. Builds social proof. Can kill leads if reviews are bad.

<PullQuote>"Hey, I'd love your help. Could you leave a quick Google review about your experience? Just search 'KIN Home on Google' and leave a star rating and a quick comment. It really helps."</PullQuote>

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "4.5+", label: "Minimum Star Rating" },
  { value: "20+", label: "Minimum Review Count" },
  { value: "ALL", label: "Respond to Every Review" }
]} />

</Step>

<Step number={2} title="Before/After Installation Photos">

Visual proof of quality. Shows progression. Builds confidence for prospects.

**Collect:** Before (existing roof, clear angle) → During (installation in progress) → After (finished system with smart meter) → Production (day one numbers).

<Callout type="tip">
Get customer permission before publishing. Most are happy — it's free advertising for them too.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step number={3} title="Facebook Business Page">

Most local customers are on Facebook. Easy to manage. Shows company legitimacy.

**Include:** Company info, professional logo, before/after photos, customer testimonials, regular posts (2-3x/week), team photos, community involvement.

**Content ideas:** "Solar Saturdays" tip posts, customer spotlights, educational content, team wins, neighborhood installs, seasonal urgency.
</Step>

<Step number={4} title="Instagram Presence">

Visual platform — perfect for before/afters. Reaches younger demographic. Easy local reach with hashtags.

**Post:** Before/after photos, team photos, production updates, testimonials, behind-the-scenes, educational graphics.

**Hashtags:** Local (#CarlsbadSolar #SanDiegoSolar), Industry (#SolarEnergy #GoSolar), Engagement (#SolarSavings #EnergyIndependence).

<KeyTerm term="Cadence">3-4 posts per week minimum.</KeyTerm>
</Step>

<Step number={5} title="Customer Testimonials (Video > Text)">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "10×", label: "Video Is More Powerful Than Written" },
  { value: "30-60s", label: "Ideal Length" },
  { value: "PHONE", label: "Quality Is Fine" }
]} />

**Prompt them:** "How has solar changed your life?" "How much are you saving?" "Would you recommend KIN?" "What surprised you?"

**Use them:** Social media, email to prospects, team meetings, sales presentations.
</Step>

<Step number={6} title="Email Newsletter (Optional but Powerful)">

Once per month. Stays in touch with past contacts. Builds authority. Drives referrals.

**Content:** Rate increase alerts, customer spotlights, team spotlights, solar education, seasonal promotions.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Digital Funnel">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Awareness (Top of Funnel)">
Facebook ads to neighborhood. Instagram posts with local hashtags. Google reviews and search presence. Word-of-mouth spreading online (tags, shares).
</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Interest (Middle of Funnel)">
Website or landing page with system details. FAQ addressing common objections. Before/after galleries. Customer testimonial videos.
</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Decision (Bottom of Funnel)">
Google reviews for social proof. Email or messenger for quote. Case study showing savings. Clear CTA to book appointment.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Social Media Rules">

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Post consistently (schedule if needed) | Post politics or divisive content |
| Engage with followers (respond to comments) | Ignore negative comments |
| Use local hashtags | Copy competitor content |
| Feature your team (humanize brand) | Post low-quality photos |
| Celebrate customer wins | Spam your followers |
| Share educational content | Make false claims |
| Be authentic | Overpromise results |
| Quality over quantity | Disappear for months |

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Leveraging Your Team">

<Callout type="important" title="EVERY TEAM MEMBER IS A BRAND AMBASSADOR">
10 team members sharing = 10x reach. Personal shares get higher engagement than company posts.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Share Company Posts">On personal Facebook — friends are more likely to engage.</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Tag KIN in Stories">When knocking doors, at installs, at team events.</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Share Customer Testimonials">From their own closures — personal connection.</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Post About Wins">Appointments set, deals closed, team celebrations.</Step>
<Step number={5} title="Use Branded Hashtags">Consistent brand identity across all personal posts.</Step>
</Steps>

<KeyTerm term="Culture Effect">This creates team pride. When everyone shares, the brand becomes bigger than any one person.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The 30-Day Digital Domination Challenge">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Week 1: Foundation">
Set up Facebook business page. Collect 5 customer testimonial videos. Take 20 before/after photos. Set up Instagram. Get 10 Google reviews.
</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Week 2: Momentum">
Post twice daily on Facebook. Post 4x on Instagram. Feature customer testimonial video on all platforms. Respond to all comments and reviews. Invite 20 customers to leave Google reviews.
</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Week 3: Amplify">
Continue daily posting. Launch email list (collect 30 emails from past contacts). Create one customer case study. Get team members sharing.
</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Week 4: Sustain">
Maintain momentum. Measure results. Double down on what's working. Plan month 2 content.
</Step>
</Steps>

<PullQuote>"Consistency beats perfection. Regular posting with authentic content will always outperform sporadic bursts of polished content. Show great work and happy customers — that's digital domination in solar sales."</PullQuote>

</Accordion>
