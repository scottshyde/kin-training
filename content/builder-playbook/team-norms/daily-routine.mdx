# Daily Routine

<Lead>"Show me your calendar and bank statement and I'll show you what you really value." — Peter Drucker. Your daily routine is your real performance system. What you do between 7am and 9pm — and in what order — determines what you're capable of at the door.</Lead>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Monday-Friday">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="7:00 AM — Wake">

<Callout type="warning">
No snooze. The day you establish that the alarm is a suggestion is the day your discipline starts slipping.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step number={2} title="7:00-8:00 AM — Power Hour">

<KeyTerm term="The Foundational Hour">This sets your state. Reps who skip it show up to their first door at a deficit.</KeyTerm>

- **Affirmations** — read or say your DMP out loud
- **Cold shower** — activates the nervous system, builds mental toughness
- **Reading** — 15-20 minutes of mindset or skill development
- **Water** — 16+ oz before anything else
- **Pack the car** — materials, iPad, charger, everything you need
</Step>

<Step number={3} title="8:00-9:00 AM — Workout">
Physical training is non-negotiable. Anything counts — gym, run, bike. Movement before the day starts.
</Step>

<Step number={4} title="9:00-10:00 AM — Meal / Shower">
Eat real food. Get ready like the day matters, because it does.
</Step>

<Step number={5} title="10:00 AM-12:00 PM — Power Hours / Pipeline">
Review weekly plan. Respond to emails and texts. Follow up with pipeline (leads, confirms, booked appointments). Pre-work for the day's knocking.
</Step>

<Step number={6} title="12:00-1:00 PM — Team Meeting (Mon/Fri)">
Attendance required. See Standards.
</Step>

<Step number={7} title="2:00-4:00 PM — Signs of Life">
Warm-up knocking. Not prime time, but not wasted. Work the easier part of your territory. Get your reps in.
</Step>

<Step number={8} title="4:00-4:30 PM — Reset">
Mental and physical reset before prime time. Brief break, food, water.
</Step>

<Step number={9} title="4:30-8:00 PM — Prime Time">

<Callout type="important" title="PROTECT THIS AT ALL COSTS">
This is the most valuable window of the day. Homeowners are home from work. Decisions get made in the evening. **No social media. No long texts. No errands.** You are at doors during this window.
</Callout>
</Step>

<Step number={10} title="8:00-8:30 PM — After Dark / 'Come Back Soon'">
Leave notes or cards for homes where no one answered during prime time. "Sorry I missed you" cards convert surprisingly well.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>
