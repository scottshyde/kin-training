# Interview Questions & Recruiting Scorecard

<Lead>Recruiting is one of the highest-leverage activities a team leader can do. One A-player who runs for three years generates more revenue and less headache than five mediocre hires. The interview is where you find them.</Lead>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Target Profile">

<KeyTerm term="Look for Athletes">Specifically: wrestlers, rowers, crew members, competitive swimmers, distance runners. People who have been trained by a coach to do something hard, in the presence of discomfort, with a team depending on them.</KeyTerm>

<Callout type="insight">
Sports build the three traits you need in a solar rep before they ever touch a door.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Three Traits">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "GRIT", label: "Will They Persist When It's Hard?" },
  { value: "PERSONABLE", label: "Can They Connect Genuinely?" },
  { value: "ACTIVATOR", label: "Do They Initiate Without Being Told?" }
]} />

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Grit Questions">

<PullQuote>"What does your daily routine look like right now?"</PullQuote>

<KeyTerm term="Looking For">Structure, intentionality, early rising, physical training.</KeyTerm>

<PullQuote>"What's an area of your life where you've been particularly disciplined?"</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"Tell me about the most challenging goal you've pursued. How long did it take? What got in the way?"</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"Tell me about a time you wanted to quit something but didn't. What kept you going?"</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"What do you think separates you from other people your age?"</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Personable Questions">

<PullQuote>"How do you adjust your communication style for different types of people?"</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"If someone came to the door and said 'not interested' — walk me through how you'd respond."</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"How do you make someone feel comfortable when you've just met them?"</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"If I called your last employer right now, what would they say about how you treat people?"</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Activator Questions">

<PullQuote>"Tell me about the biggest personal goal you've ever set for yourself. What did you sacrifice to pursue it?"</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"On a scale of 1-10, how lucky are you in life? Explain your answer."</PullQuote>

<Callout type="insight">
High performers almost always answer 8-10 and **credit their own choices**. Low performers answer 4-6 and attribute outcomes to circumstance.
</Callout>

<PullQuote>"Give me an example of a time you did something completely outside the box to solve a problem."</PullQuote>

<PullQuote>"If I asked you to rank family, finances, health, relationships, and personal development in order of how much you actually invest in them right now — not how you wish you did — what would that order be?"</PullQuote>

<KeyTerm term="What This Reveals">Self-awareness. Honest, reflective answers are the goal.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Red Flags">

<Callout type="warning" title="END THE INTERVIEW EARLY IF YOU SEE">
These aren't character judgments — they're hiring filters. You're looking for a specific profile.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="No Transportation">Can't get to doors.</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Many Short-Tenure Jobs">Pattern of quitting.</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Poor Hygiene / Disheveled">First impression matters.</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Timid Energy">Soft handshake, avoiding eye contact.</Step>
<Step number={5} title="Short, One-Word Answers">No depth of thought.</Step>
<Step number={6} title="No Competitive History">Zero sports, debate, or anything competitive.</Step>
<Step number={7} title="Entitlement Language">"I expect," "I deserve," "I was owed."</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Recruiting Scorecard">

| Trait | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|-------|---|---|---|---|
| **Grit** | No evidence | One example | Consistent pattern | Exceptional |
| **Personable** | Awkward/cold | Adequate | Naturally warm | Magnetic |
| **Activator** | Waits to be told | Follows willingly | Often initiates | Relentlessly self-directed |

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Part-Time Exceptions">

<Callout type="warning">
For part-time candidates, the bar doesn't lower — the schedule accommodates.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="All Weekly Meetings">Attendance required.</Step>
<Step number={2} title="One Full Weekend Day">Non-negotiable.</Step>
<Step number={3} title="4-6 Productive Hours/Week Minimum">Bare minimum.</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Score 7-9 on Scorecard">Higher bar, not lower.</Step>
</Steps>

<KeyTerm term="The Rule">Partial commitment with a low score is a recipe for a rep who consumes attention without producing results.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>
