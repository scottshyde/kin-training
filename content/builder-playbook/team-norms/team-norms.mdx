# Team Norms: The Standards That Build Great Teams

<Lead>Team norms are the unwritten rules and standards of excellence that define how your team operates. They're not rules you're forced to follow — they're agreements you make with your teammates about what kind of team you want to be.</Lead>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 1: Accountability Always">

<KeyTerm term="What It Means">Own your results. Don't make excuses. Acknowledge mistakes quickly. Commit publicly to goals. Follow through on what you say.</KeyTerm>

<Callout type="insight">
Teams that own results outperform teams that make excuses. Your accountability makes everyone around you better.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 2: Daily Door Discipline">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "10:30", label: "At First Door Before This" },
  { value: "MON-SAT", label: "Every Day, On Time" }
]} />

<KeyTerm term="The Standard">Prepared. Professional dress. Confidence. Show up even when tired. Don't call in sick to dodge a difficult week. Consistency beats talent.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 3: System Execution Over Improvisation">

<Callout type="warning">
The system is proven. Memorize word tracks — don't freestyle them. Follow the solidification process — don't skip steps. Use the objection playsets — don't wing it.
</Callout>

<KeyTerm term="Innovation Rule">If you think you have a better way, test it with your manager first. Innovation happens in coaching sessions, not at the door.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 4: Coachability">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Receive Feedback">Without defensiveness.</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Ask for Help">When you're stuck.</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Implement Quickly">Don't just hear it — do it.</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Report Back">Close the loop on coaching.</Step>
</Steps>

<KeyTerm term="The Truth">Your best growth comes from feedback. Defensive people don't improve. Coachable people become top performers.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 5: Celebrating Wins">

<Callout type="insight">
Celebration builds momentum. Your wins are team wins. Acknowledgment builds culture. Celebrating others costs nothing and gains everything.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 6: Zero Gossip Culture">

<Callout type="warning" title="THE STANDARD">
If you have an issue with someone, talk to them or your manager. Don't complain about a teammate to another teammate. Don't participate in negative conversations.
</Callout>

<PullQuote>"I don't think that's fair — talk to them directly."</PullQuote>

<KeyTerm term="Why">Gossip destroys team culture. Trust is built on directness and honesty. Great teams have low drama.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 7: Lead By Example">

<KeyTerm term="The Only Leadership That Works">People do what you do, not what you say. Culture flows from the top. Your credibility comes from walking the talk.</KeyTerm>

You're not just asking others to hit door goals — you're hitting them first. Your scripts are tight because you practiced. You handle rejection well, so your team handles rejection well.

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 8: Radical Honesty">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Tell the Truth">Even when it's uncomfortable.</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Don't Sugarcoat Results">The numbers are what they are.</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Admit Mistakes Immediately">Problems hidden become bigger problems.</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Say 'I Don't Understand'">When you don't.</Step>
</Steps>

<KeyTerm term="The Principle">Honesty builds trust faster than anything else. You can't improve what you won't acknowledge. Truth allows the team to help you.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 9: Never Settle for Mediocrity">

<Callout type="important">
Good is not enough. "We'll try our best" is not our standard. Excellence is a habit, not a moment. Your standard sets the team's standard.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Norm 10: Support Each Other">

<KeyTerm term="Not Competing Against Each Other">We're competing for the customer. Share leads if it helps the team. Cover for someone struggling. Mentor newer reps. Be genuinely happy about each other's success.</KeyTerm>

<Callout type="insight">
A rising tide lifts all boats. Supporting others creates a culture where you're also supported.
</Callout>

</Accordion>
