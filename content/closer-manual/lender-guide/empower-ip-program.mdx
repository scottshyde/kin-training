# Empower IP Program

<Lead>The Empower IP (Installer Program) is one of the financing/partnership structures KIN operates under. Understanding its mechanics helps set accurate expectations on deal economics and payout timing.</Lead>

<PullQuote>"We are in the minor leagues." — Use this as a mindset frame. The program has guardrails. Learn them. Work within them. Build toward more leverage over time.</PullQuote>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Holdbacks">

<KeyTerm term="What They Are">Empower retains holdbacks on deals — amounts kept in reserve until conditions are met. These are not forfeited; they're deferred.</KeyTerm>

<Callout type="warning">
Know the holdback structure before committing to rep payouts on deals going through this program.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Platform Fee">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "$0.20/W", label: "Platform Fee" },
  { value: "WAIVED", label: "Current Status" }
]} />

<Callout type="tip">
Currently waived — but treat it as a potential cost in deal modeling if that changes.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Equipment Sourcing">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="SNRGY Supply">
Used for domestic content qualification under the Sunrun side of the program.
</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Everbright / Greentech">
Quote using the **Safe Harbor AVL (Approved Vendor List)**.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Don't quote off-list equipment for Empower deals — it creates compliance issues downstream.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Milestone Payment Schedule">

<KeyTerm term="Pay-When-Paid Model">KIN receives payment when Empower receives payment from its capital source.</KeyTerm>

| Milestone | Payment |
|-----------|---------|
| Installation complete | **80%** |
| PTO (Permission to Operate) | **20%** |

<Callout type="tip">
Expect a **1-week delay** after each milestone is approved before funds arrive. Plan cash flow accordingly.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Credit Requirements">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "650", label: "Minimum FICO Score" }
]} />

<Callout type="important">
Deals with homeowners below 650 will not be funded through Empower. **Qualify credit before running a full proposal** to avoid wasted effort and homeowner disappointment.
</Callout>

<KeyTerm term="Soft Pulls">Soft credit pulls are available and don't impact the homeowner's score. Always run the soft pull before design.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Positioning to Homeowners">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Established Financing">
The financing is underwritten by an established company.
</Step>

Initial check won't affect their score.

Simple process for qualifying homeowners.
</Step>

Nothing final until they review and sign documents.
</Step>
</Steps>

<KeyTerm term="Keep It Simple">Keep the homeowner conversation focused on their outcome, not your back-end mechanics.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Operational Notes">

<Callout type="warning" title="FLAG EARLY">
Flag Empower deals to operations early — equipment sourcing and AVL compliance need lead time.
</Callout>

- PTO delays from the utility are outside KIN's control — set homeowner expectations at signing
- Holdback disputes are handled through Empower's dealer relations team — **document everything**

</Accordion>
