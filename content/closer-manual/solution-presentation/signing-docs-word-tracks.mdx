# Signing Docs Word Tracks

<Lead>The signing phase is where the deal becomes real. Your customer has committed verbally — now they need to sign the HIA and related documents. This is where last-minute doubts surface. Your job: walk them through with confidence, clarity, and reassurance.</Lead>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Pre-Signing Mindset & Setup">

<Callout type="important" title="FRAME THIS RIGHT">
You've already earned the business through your needs audit and solution presentation. The documents are simply formalizing what you've already agreed to. This is **not** a sales conversation — this is an administrative process.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Mental Preparation">
Your confidence transfers to the customer. This is routine and expected.
</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Physical Setup">
Documents organized. Smooth pen. Sit **beside** the customer — collaborative, not adversarial. Clean folder ready for their copy.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Opening Word Track">

<PullQuote>"Jimmy, we're going to take a few minutes and walk through some paperwork. I know paperwork can seem intimidating, but I promise you there are no surprises here. Everything we're signing today is exactly what we've already discussed. These documents just formalize our agreement and protect both you and KIN."</PullQuote>

<KeyTerm term="What This Accomplishes">Acknowledges potential anxiety → Sets expectations → Confirms this is normal → Promises clarity and protection.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Walking Through the HIA">

### Section 1: Customer Information & System Details

<PullQuote>"First, let's make sure all your information is correct. Your name, address, phone number, email. I'm going to send you updates here throughout the project, so make sure this email is the one you check regularly. Here's the system we're installing — this is what our team engineered specifically for your home."</PullQuote>

### Section 2: System Cost & Financing

**PPA Word Track:**

<PullQuote>"You selected our PPA option, which is perfect for your situation. Zero dollars down today. We own and maintain the system for 25 years. You pay a reduced rate for the solar power you use. The rate increases 3.5% annually — significantly less than SDGE's increases."</PullQuote>

**Loan/Cash Word Track:**

<PullQuote>"Your total investment is $X. With the loan, you own the system immediately — which means you qualify for the federal tax credit of 30%. Your monthly payment is $X, which is less than your current SDGE bill. After the loan term, you own it free and clear."</PullQuote>

### Section 3: Warranty Coverage

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "25yr", label: "Product — panels & inverter" },
  { value: "25yr", label: "Workmanship — every wire, every penetration" },
  { value: "25yr", label: "Performance — minimum 80% rated capacity" },
  { value: "10yr", label: "Battery backup (if applicable)" }
]} />

<PullQuote>"This is one of the best warranties in the industry, and it's why customers choose KIN. You're protected."</PullQuote>

### Section 4: Timeline & Next Steps

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Week 1-2: Design">
Our design team finalizes the exact electrical layout and roof design.
</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Week 3: Permits">
We submit to the city. Typically takes 4-6 weeks.
</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Week 7-10: Installation">
Once permits clear, we schedule your install date.
</Step>
<Step number={4} title="After Installation">
We handle SDGE interconnection approval — you don't have to do anything.
</Step>
</Steps>

<PullQuote>"The total timeline from today to your system being live is typically 8-12 weeks. You'll get updates from our team throughout — no surprises."</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Handling Last-Minute Concerns">

### "The Price Seems High"

<PullQuote>"Let me show you the context. The average cost per watt in San Diego is $2.50 to $3.00. We're competitive — and you're getting the best equipment, 25-year warranties, and in-house installation. You're not paying for a middleman. You're paying for quality."</PullQuote>

### "I Don't Want the System Touching My Roof"

<PullQuote>"Our installation process is engineered to protect your roof. Roof-rated fasteners sealed with premium flashing. Our team has installed thousands of systems with zero roof leaks. A properly installed solar system can actually add to your roof's value and longevity."</PullQuote>

### "What If My SDGE Bill Goes Down?"

<PullQuote>"With a PPA, you only pay for the power you use from solar. If your bill goes down because of conservation, your solar bill goes down proportionally. You always get the benefit."</PullQuote>

### "When Will I See the Savings?"

<PullQuote>"You'll see savings immediately — from the first bill. Instead of paying SDGE their full rate, you're paying us our reduced rate. By Year 3, your savings will be dramatic. By Year 25, you'll have saved tens of thousands. This is a long-game investment in your family's financial future."</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Signing Sequence">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Walkthrough Complete">

<PullQuote>"Does everything here make sense? Are there any questions before we finalize?"</PullQuote>
</Step>

<Step number={2} title="Commitment Confirmation">

<PullQuote>"Perfect. You're making a great decision for your family. Let's get this signed."</PullQuote>
</Step>

<Step number={3} title="Signing Instructions">
Point to each signature and initial line. Date it. Same from spouse if applicable.
</Step>

<Step number={4} title="Document Review">

<PullQuote>"Here's your copy — keep this somewhere safe. You'll receive a digital copy by email within 24 hours as well."</PullQuote>
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<Callout type="important" title="DON'T JUST SAY GOODBYE">
Reinforce the decision and set expectations.

<PullQuote>"Jimmy, I want you to know — you made the right decision today. You just protected your family from rising energy costs and invested in clean energy. Our team is going to take great care of you from here. If you have any questions in the meantime, reach out to me directly. I'm here for you."</PullQuote>
