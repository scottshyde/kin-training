# Path Progression

<Lead>Mastery in solar sales isn't random. It follows a sequence. The rep who tries to learn everything at once learns nothing well. The rep who builds skills in the right order develops a foundation that compounds.</Lead>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Stage 1: The Fundamentals (Weeks 1-4)">

<Callout type="important" title="MASTER THESE FIRST">
These four are the engine. Every other technique in this training assumes you have them. If your case open is shaky or your needs audit feels scripted, work here first.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Case Open">
The 60-word pitch.
</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Needs Audit">
The discovery sequence.
</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Paraverbal Communication">
Tone, pace, volume.
</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Nonverbal Communication">
Positioning, eye contact, reading cues.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Stage 2: Objection Handling (Weeks 3-6)">

<Steps>
<Step number={5} title="Smokescreens">
Surface objections and how to move past them.
</Step>
<Step number={6} title="Solving on the Door">
Finding the Black Swan.
</Step>
<Step number={7} title="Additional Ammo">
Reframes, vocabulary, unexpected situations.
</Step>
<Step number={8} title="SDGE Story">
The hypothetical close.
</Step>
</Steps>

<KeyTerm term="Volume Matters">You won't encounter objections at a meaningful rate until your door count is high enough. Push your volume during this stage so you get enough reps to actually practice.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Stage 3: Appointment Quality (Weeks 5-8)">

<Steps>
<Step number={9} title="Solidifying the Appointment">
The option close, rapport building.
</Step>
<Step number={10} title="Stickier Appointments">
Softball questions, re-entry audit.
</Step>
<Step number={11} title="Text Templates">
The confirmation system.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="insight">
Your closing percentage means nothing if your appointments don't show. This stage is about converting booked leads into **real sits**.
</Callout>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Stage 4: Advanced Technique (Weeks 7-10)">

<Steps>
<Step number={12} title="Drill Down Questions">
Going deeper in any conversation.
</Step>
<Step number={13} title="Types of Questions">
Deploying the right question at the right moment.
</Step>
<Step number={14} title="Maintain Control in Needs Audit">
Handling premature questions.
</Step>
<Step number={15} title="Powerful Position Statements">
Differentiating KIN with confidence.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Stage 5: Expansion (Week 10+)">

<Steps>
<Step number={16} title="Knocking an Install">
Turning completions into referral pipelines.
</Step>
<Step number={17} title="Lead Calling">
Working the phone alongside the door.
</Step>
<Step number={18} title="Door Pitch HVAC">
Adding the second product line.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="What Accelerates Everything">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "60", label: "Doors/Day Compresses 12 Weeks → 6" }
]} />

<KeyTerm term="The Accelerator">Door count. Every stage progresses faster with more reps. You cannot think your way to skill. You have to knock your way there.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Open Door Policy (First 3 Weeks)">

<Callout type="tip">
New reps are required to attend **open office hours** during the first three weeks after bootcamp. This is the time to ask questions, review recordings, and get direct coaching while the fundamentals are fresh.
</Callout>

Use it.

</Accordion>
