# Schedule Them — Outbound Text

<Lead>Before you knock a street, a well-timed text to warm contacts in that area can prime the block and convert appointments before you even knock. This technique works when you have a number from a prior interaction — a neighbor referral, a previous conversation, or an inbound lead who didn't book.</Lead>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="The Template">

<PullQuote>"Morning [Name], hope you had a solid rest of your week. We just helped your neighbor get rolling with our new Solar and Battery options. We're going to be right down the street today at 1 — if we swung by after that, would you be around to see what we can do to get rid of SDGE? I'm confident it'll be worth your time."</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Why This Works">

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="'Morning [Name]'">
Specific greeting, their name, reads like a real person texting.
</Step>

<Step number={2} title="'Hope you had a solid rest of your week'">
Warm without being sycophantic. Grounds it in modern casual conversation.
</Step>

<Step number={3} title="'We just helped your neighbor...'">
Immediate social proof. Unnamed neighbor keeps it compliant while signaling activity in the area.
</Step>

<Step number={4} title="'Right down the street today at 1'">
Creates immediacy and physical proximity. This isn't a future thing — it's **today**.
</Step>

<Step number={5} title="'Would you be around'">
Low-barrier ask. Not "can we book an appointment" — just "are you around." The barrier to yes is almost nothing.
</Step>

<Step number={6} title="'I'm confident it'll be worth your time'">
Confident without being pushy. Keeps the tone relaxed.
</Step>
</Steps>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="When to Send It">

<StatBlock stats={[
  { value: "8-10 AM", label: "Morning of — Ideal Window" },
  { value: "NOT", label: "Night Before — Too Far Out" },
  { value: "NOT", label: "After Noon — Too Short" }
]} />

<KeyTerm term="Why Morning-Of">The urgency of "today" is the entire engine. Too early and it fades. Too late and the window is gone.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Follow the Text with the Knock">

<Callout type="insight">
If they don't respond, **still knock the door.** The text primed them — even an unread text creates a subliminal "oh I think I saw something from them" effect when you show up. You're no longer completely cold.
</Callout>

If they do respond positively, that's an almost-booked appointment before you've left your car.

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />
