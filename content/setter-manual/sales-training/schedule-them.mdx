# Schedule Them — Outbound Text

Before you knock a street, a well-timed text to warm contacts in that area can prime the block and convert appointments before you even knock.

This technique works when you have a number from a prior interaction — a neighbor referral, a previous conversation, or an inbound lead who didn't book.

---

## The Template

> *"Morning [Name], hope you had a solid rest of your week 🫡 We just helped your neighbor get rolling with our new Solar and Battery options. We're going to be right down the street today at 1 — if we swung by after that, would you be around to see what we can do to get rid of SDGE? I'm confident it'll be worth your time 🤙"*

---

## Why This Works

**"Morning [Name]"** — Specific greeting, their name, reads like a real person texting.

**"Hope you had a solid rest of your week 🫡"** — Warm without being sycophantic. The emoji grounds it in modern casual conversation.

**"We just helped your neighbor..."** — Immediate social proof. Unnamed neighbor (keeping it compliant) signals activity in the area without overcommitting.

**"Right down the street today at 1"** — Creates immediacy and physical proximity. You're not far away. This isn't a future thing — it's today.

**"Would you be around"** — Low-barrier ask. Not "can we book an appointment" — just "are you around." The barrier to saying yes is almost nothing.

**"I'm confident it'll be worth your time 🤙"** — Confident without being pushy. The 🤙 keeps the tone relaxed.

---

## When to Send It

- **Morning of** — send between 8 and 10am on the day you'll be in that area
- **Not the night before** — that's too far out and the urgency of "today" is lost
- **Not day-of past noon** — cuts the window too short

---

## Follow the Text with the Knock

If they don't respond, still knock the door. The text primed them — even an unread text creates a subliminal "oh I think I saw something from them" effect when you show up. You're no longer completely cold.

If they do respond positively, that's an almost-booked appointment before you've left your car.

---

## Volume

Send 10–20 of these on a focused morning before a productive afternoon shift. Even a 15% response rate produces 2–3 warm conversations in an area you're already planning to work.

Stack the text campaign with the knocking campaign — same territory, same day.
