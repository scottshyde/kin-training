# Text Templates — Self-Gen

<Lead>Self-gen leads are your highest-value pipeline. You found them, you qualified them, you own the relationship. These templates maintain that warmth from the door to the sit.</Lead>

<Accordion title="Booked — With Bill">

<PullQuote>"Hey [Name]! It was great meeting you today. Confirming your appointment for [Day] at [Time]. Based on your bill, I think the numbers are going to look really solid for you. See you then! — David West, Your Energy Best"</PullQuote>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Booked — No Bill">

<PullQuote>"Hey [Name] — David here from KIN Home. Great chatting with you! Confirming [Day] at [Time]. One thing that would help: if you can log into myenergycenter.com and screenshot your usage page, I can make sure we show you the most accurate numbers. Looking forward to it! — David West, Your Energy Best"</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Interested — No Booking">

<KeyTerm term="When to Use">They were warm but didn't commit to a time.</KeyTerm>

<PullQuote>"Hey [Name] — David from KIN. Really enjoyed talking with you today. I know you wanted a bit more time to think, but I didn't want to close out my schedule without giving you the chance to see the numbers. I've got [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time] — either one work? — David West, Your Energy Best"</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Follow-Up (No Response)">

<PullQuote>"Hey [Name] — just following up from our conversation [day]. No pressure at all — just want to make sure you have the chance to see what we put together before the week closes out. Worth 20 minutes if you're curious. — David West, Your Energy Best"</PullQuote>

</Accordion>

<GoldRule />

<Accordion title="Tone Notes for Self-Gen">

<Callout type="insight" title="MORE PERSONAL THAN COMPANY LEADS">
Self-gen texts should feel slightly more personal. You met this person. They know your name. The text should reflect that.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step number={1} title="Use Their Name">
Personal, not templated.
</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Reference the Conversation">
Mention something specific from the door if you can.
</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Don't Over-Explain">
The warmth was built at the door — the text just maintains it.
</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Keep It Brief and Human">
Short sentences. Real language. No corporate tone.
</Step>
</Steps>

<KeyTerm term="Your Energy Best">The sign-off is intentional — it's memorable and warm without being salesy.</KeyTerm>

</Accordion>
