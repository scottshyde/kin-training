# Text Templates — Setter

These templates are your communication layer between the door and the sit. Send them within 30 minutes of any door interaction — while the conversation is still fresh in both directions.

**Your Popl card:** https://popl.co/card/xtN92upV/9/s
**Bill lookup:** myenergycenter.com → Billing → view detailed bill PDF

<Accordion title="Booked — No Bill">
When you've booked the appointment but couldn't pull the bill:

*"Hey [Name]! It was great meeting you today. I'm confirming your appointment for [Day] at [Time] with my manager [Manager Name]. To make sure we can give you the most accurate numbers, can you log in to myenergycenter.com and grab your most recent bill? You can just screenshot the usage page and text it to me. See you [Day]!"*
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Booked — With Bill">
When you have the bill and the appointment is set:

*"Hey [Name]! Great chatting with you today. Confirming your appointment for [Day] at [Time] — my manager [Manager Name] will be coming by. Everything looks good with what I pulled on your bill. See you then!"*
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Booked — With Roof, No Bill">
When roof concerns came up but they booked anyway:

*"Hey [Name] — Dave from KIN Home. Great talking with you. Confirming [Day] at [Time] — [Manager Name] will be coming by and he's actually seen a lot of roof situations like yours. He'll be able to tell you right away whether it's a factor or not. See you then!"*
</Accordion>

<Tab>

*"Hey [Name] — Dave here from KIN Home. Really enjoyed talking with you today. I know you wanted to think about it, but I wanted to make sure you at least had the chance to see the numbers before I close out my schedule for the week. I've got [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time] — either of those work?"*
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Interested — Got Bill, No Booking">
When they pulled the bill but still didn't book:

*"Hey [Name] — I ran your bill numbers on the way back to my car and honestly they look really good for you. You'd be looking at roughly [estimate] a month vs what you're paying now. Worth 20 minutes with my manager to confirm that. [Day] at [Time] work?"*
</Accordion>
</Tab>

Day-before confirmation when you have bill in hand:

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Confirm — No Bill">
Day-before confirmation without bill:

*"Hey [Name] — confirming for tomorrow at [Time] with [Manager Name]. One thing that would help us a lot: if you have a second tonight, log into myenergycenter.com and text me a screenshot of your billing page. Makes a huge difference in the accuracy of what we show you. See you tomorrow!"*
</Accordion>

## Tone Notes

- Use their name at the start
- Keep it warm but not over-eager
- Always include a specific time and the manager's name
- Give them a reason to respond (question or CTA)
- Sign off with your name the first time, then just your first name after
